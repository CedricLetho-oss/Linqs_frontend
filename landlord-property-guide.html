Property Listing Guide

Create compelling listings that attract quality tenants

High-Quality Photos

Properties with professional photos get 3x more views and bookings.

  • Use natural lighting
  • Capture all rooms and amenities
  • Include exterior shots
  • Show nearby attractions

Compelling Descriptions

Write descriptions that highlight unique features and benefits.

  • Start with an engaging headline
  • Highlight key amenities
  • Describe the neighborhood
  • Mention transportation options
Pro Tip: Complete Your Profile

Landlords with complete profiles and verified identities receive 40% more booking inquiries. Make sure to fill out all sections of your landlord profile.

Property Management Tips

Effective strategies for managing your rental properties

Booking Management
  • Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
  • Use the calendar to block unavailable dates
  • Set clear check-in/check-out times
  • Create house rules for tenants
Tenant Communication
  • Be responsive to tenant messages
  • Provide clear instructions for check-in
  • Send reminders before arrival
  • Request feedback after stay
Maintenance
  • Schedule regular property inspections
  • Address maintenance issues promptly
  • Keep a record of repairs and upgrades
  • Prepare for seasonal maintenance
Review Management
  • Encourage tenants to leave reviews
  • Respond professionally to all reviews
  • Address negative feedback constructively
  • Use feedback to improve your property

Pricing Strategy

Optimize your rental rates for maximum profitability

Setting the right price is crucial for attracting tenants while maximizing your rental income. Consider these factors:

  • Research comparable properties in your area
  • Adjust prices based on seasonality and demand
  • Offer discounts for longer stays
  • Consider including or excluding utilities
  • Review and adjust pricing regularly

Promotion Tips

Increase visibility and attract more tenants

Promote your property effectively to reach more potential tenants:

  • Share your listing on social media
  • Use high-quality, professional photos
  • Highlight unique features and amenities
  • Offer special promotions for first-time tenants
  • Ask satisfied tenants for referrals

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Solutions for common property management challenges

If your property isn't receiving enough bookings:

  • Review and improve your photos
  • Adjust your pricing strategy
  • Enhance your property description
  • Consider offering promotions
  • Ensure your calendar is up to date

For improving communication with tenants:

  • Set clear expectations from the beginning
  • Respond to messages promptly
  • Use our messaging templates
  • Provide multiple contact methods
  • Send automated reminders for check-in/out

For handling maintenance issues:

  • Create a network of reliable contractors
  • Schedule regular property inspections
  • Address issues promptly to prevent escalation
  • Keep records of all maintenance activities
  • Set aside funds for unexpected repairs
