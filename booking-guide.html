Booking Timeline

Understand the typical timeline from search to move-in.

Week 1: Research & Shortlisting

Browse properties, compare options, and create your shortlist of preferred accommodations.

Week 2: Viewings & Applications

Schedule and attend viewings, then submit applications for your top choices.

Week 3: Approval & Documentation

Receive approval, complete paperwork, and submit required deposits.

Week 4: Preparation & Move-in

Finalize arrangements, set up utilities, and prepare for move-in day.

Pro Tip: Start your search 4-6 weeks before your intended move-in date to allow ample time for viewings and processing.
