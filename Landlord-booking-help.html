Troubleshooting Common Issues

Quick solutions for frequent booking and payment problems

If you haven't received a payment:

  • Check the booking status - payment is released 24 hours after check-in
  • Verify your payout method in account settings
  • Ensure your banking details are correct
  • Contact support if payment is delayed beyond 3 business days

To prevent double bookings:

  • Always update your calendar immediately after accepting a booking
  • Use the calendar sync feature with external calendars
  • Set a buffer period between bookings for cleaning/maintenance
  • Enable instant booking only if you can manage rapid responses

If commission calculations seem incorrect:

  • Verify which commission plan you're currently on
  • Check if the booking was affected by any promotions
  • Review the detailed breakdown in your transaction history
  • Contact support for a detailed explanation of any charge
